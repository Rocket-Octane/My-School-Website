Birchbank Public School

My school focuses on the education of its students. Along with learning, my school also includes clubs and fun activities both online and in school. We have very helpful and kind students here who are always willing to help their peers.

Campus Life

Minecraft Clubs

Was started at December 7th, 2021.

In the Minecraft clubs we get to create buildings, mazes, traps, etc. We also get to try out different modes that we would like to make.



Concert Area

for concerts

Each year we have concerts for spring and winter and this is where we perform what we have practised. Each class does there own seperately, but there are some chances of two classes of the same grade performing together.



Library

Has lots of genres of books

Every week we can choose one book to take out to read at home and at school. There are various genres of books in our library, from comedy to fantasy. You can find almost any genre you like from any library, even if it is a public library.

Different Types of Classes

Music
Has music 2 times a week

In music, we learn all about the notes and how to play different instruments, their musical families and their lines and spaces. WE HAVE A VERY KIND TEACHER WHO TEACHES US THESE THINGS.

Phys Ed
Has Phys Ed periods 2 times a week

In Phys Ed, we do some cardio, stretching along with playing some games that include cardio and stretching. WE HAVE A VERY SWEET AND UNDERSTANDING TEACHER WHO HELPS US IN KEEPING US GOING AND KEEPING US ENTHUSIASTIC ABOUT DOING EXERCISES.

French
Has french for grade 4's and 5's every day

In french, we learn all about the basic words and how to phrase and structure our sentences in French. If you didn't know, French is the second major language in Canada, North America. WE HAVE A VERY HELPFUL TEACHER WHO TEACHES US THESE THINGS.
