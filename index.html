Birchbank Public School

My school focuses on the education of its students. Along with learning, my school also includes clubs and fun activities both online and in school. We have very helpful and kind students here who are always willing to help their peers.

Campus Life

Minecraft Clubs

Was started at December 7th, 2021.

In the Minecraft clubs we get to create building, mazes, traps, etc. We also get to try out different modes that we would like to make.



Concert Area

for concerts

Each year we have concerts for spring and winter and this is where we perform what we have practised. Each class does there own seperately, but there are some chances of two classes of the same grade performing together.



Library

Has lots of genres of books

Every week we can choose one book to take out to read at home and at school. There are various genres of books in our library, from comedy to fantasy. You can find almost any genre you like from any library, even if it is a public library.
